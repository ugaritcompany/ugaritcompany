Company Activities

Comprehensive construction and restoration services

UGARIT Company for Construction specializes in comprehensive construction and restoration services across Syria. Our core activities include heritage building restoration, modern construction projects, premium finishing works, technical installations, engineering consultancy, and sustainable energy solutions. We serve religious institutions, government projects, and private clients with equal dedication. Our expertise spans from delicate restoration of centuries-old structures to contemporary commercial developments, always maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and professional excellence.

Leadership

Ramez Abdalnour Zaza

Founder & Director, UGARIT Company for Construction

With over 20 years of distinguished experience in construction management and heritage restoration, Ramez Zaza has led UGARIT to become a trusted name in Syrian construction. His expertise combines traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation, overseeing major projects including the Saint Ananias Shrine restoration and serving as the accredited engineering consultant for the Syriac Catholic Archdiocese in Damascus.

A graduate of Tishreen University's Faculty of Economics, Ramez brings both business acumen and technical expertise to every project, ensuring exceptional quality and client satisfaction.
